# Code of Conduct

## Introduction

This project is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of their background, identity, or personal circumstances. This Code of Conduct outlines the expectations for behavior in our community to ensure that everyone can contribute to a positive and respectful environment.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat everyone with respect and kindness. Be considerate of different perspectives and experiences.
- **Be Supportive**: Offer help and constructive feedback. Encourage others and support their learning and growth.
- **Communicate Clearly**: Be clear and concise in your communication. Avoid language that could be misunderstood or that might come across as disrespectful.
- **Act Responsibly**: Follow through on commitments and be reliable in your contributions.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, including but not limited to, comments related to identity, personal attacks, or unwanted attention.
- **Discrimination**: Excluding or treating individuals unfairly based on personal attributes or identity.
- **Abuse of Power**: Using your position or authority to intimidate, coerce, or take advantage of others.
- **Trolling**: Engaging in behavior intended to provoke or upset others.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers. Reports can be made through the following channels:

- **Email**: attila.tolnai@student.ehb.be
- **GitHub Issues**: https://github.com/attilatolnai

Reports will be handled with confidentiality and respect. All reports will be investigated, and appropriate actions will be taken to address the situation.

## Consequences

Participants who do not adhere to this Code of Conduct may face consequences, including but not limited to:

- **Warning**: A formal warning about the behavior and a request to correct it.
- **Temporary Ban**: A temporary suspension from participating in the project.
- **Permanent Ban**: A permanent removal from the project community.

The severity of the consequences will depend on the nature of the violation and its impact on the community.

## Acknowledgment

By participating in this project, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Code of Conduct and agree to adhere to its principles.

Thank you for contributing to a positive and inclusive community!
